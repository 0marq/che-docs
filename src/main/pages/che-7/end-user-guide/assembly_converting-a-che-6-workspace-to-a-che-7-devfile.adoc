= Converting a Che 6 Workspace to a Che 7 devfile

:context: converting-a-che-6-workspace-to-a-che-7-devfile

This document describes the steps to manually convert an old Che 6 workspace configuration into a Che 7 devfile. The following are the benefits of a Che 7 devfile:

* Using an original  Che 7 devfile instead of a Che 6 factory
* A human-readable YAML format for all content of devfil.
